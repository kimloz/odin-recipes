Chai Pumpkin Spice Latte

Description

"Pumpkin Spice Chai Latte" recipe taken from www.ohhowcivilized.com

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 chai tea bags
  • 4 Tbsps pumpkin puree
  • 1 Tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/8 tsp ground cloves
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg

Steps

  1. Heat pumpkin puree and spices on low heat. In a saucepan, stir together pumpkin puree, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and nutmeg. Use a spoon to mash the ingredients.
  2. Add milk, brown sugar, and chai tea bags. Simmer on low for five minutes then take off heat. Discard tea bags and add vanilla extract. If you're using loose tea, put the tea into a tea filter bag. Keep stirring while on the stovetop so that pumpkin puree is completely incorporated into the milk and the sugar has dissolved.
  3. Froth pumpkin spice chai latte. Pour drink into an electric milk frother or French press to froth. To froth in a French press, fill it halfway with the latte and pump the plunger up and down a few times until the drink doubles in volume.
  4. Pour latte into two cups.
